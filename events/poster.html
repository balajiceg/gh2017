Poster Presentation

It is widely known that Visual expression is superior to both verbal or vocal expressions. Have you got the knack at expressing things innovatively? Then Join us this Geohorizon’17 and put your creativity to work. The topics for the presentation are related to the theme of the event
“Geospatial Technologies for Smart Infrastructure”.

Topics

  • GIS and remote sensing in Planning and Development of Smart cities.
  • An Information Framework for creating a Smart City through Internet of Things (IOT).
  • An idea for Intelligent Traffic Management.
  • Big Data and GIS empowering Connected World with the Geospatial Efficient Technologies.
  • GIS based Digital Taxation.
  • Geoinformatics for Precision Farming.
  • GIS for Crime and Accident Analysis.
  • Geospatial approach in our daily life.
  • Indoor Positioning System with Wi-Fi and Beacons.
  • Spatial Crowd Sourcing of Data.

Rules

  • Poster should be presented related to one of the topics mentioned above.
  • A team of 2 can participate for the presentation.
  • An Abstract should be submitted on time (before 7th march) ghevents17@gmail.comregarding any of the above mentioned topics.
  • Abstract should contain maximum 200 words
  • Presentation must not exceed 10 minutes.
  • Poster should be presented in A3 sheet.
  • Marks will be allotted based on creativity,content,presentation and clarity of expression.
  • The decision of judge is final.
