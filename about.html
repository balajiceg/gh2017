Society of Geo Informatics Engineers

" Success is where preparation and opportunity meet." -Bobby Unser

Society of Geoinformatics Engineers is a coterie of exhuberant minds from Institute of Remote Sensing who firmly believe in promoting the geospatial techonolgy to serve the mankind via their inputs through various projects, workshops and other activites.

This agog team with the guidance of faculities from IRS organises an intra-college and an inter-college national level symposium which provides the perfect platform for the recent innovation and developments in the domain to bloom over and reach out to the students.
